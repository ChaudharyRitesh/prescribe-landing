# Privacy & Consent — Patient Portal

Privacy-by-design, not a trailing checkbox.

## Flows
Privacy notice · purpose-specific consent · consent history · withdrawal ·
data-access info · correction requests · account-data update · erasure
(where legally/clinically permissible) · retention explanations · grievance ·
support escalation · download/export requests · sharing history · active
sessions/devices.

## Honest limits (policy-driven UI)
Not all records are deletable — clinical/financial/legal/regulatory retention
applies. UI + backend responses must explain, never over-promise.

## Consent lifecycle (patient + ABDM)
requested → granted | denied → active → expired | revoked. Each record links a
`consentRef` where relevant; revocation propagates and is reflected in UI.

## Data-rights handling
Requests are tracked workflows with status, not instant actions. Step-up
verification for sharing, export, consent changes, contact changes.

> Legal review required before publishing notices / enabling erasure / ABDM.
